# Food Tracking Guide

## Macro Calories

| Nutrient | Calories per gram | Typical ratio |
|---|---:|---:|
| Protein | 4 kcal | 25-35% |
| Carbohydrates | 4 kcal | 40-50% |
| Fat | 9 kcal | 20-30% |

## Protein Targets by Goal

| Goal | Daily intake |
|---|---|
| cut | 1.6-2.2 g/kg bodyweight |
| bulk | 1.6-2.0 g/kg bodyweight |
| maintain | 1.2-1.6 g/kg bodyweight |

## Common Food Calorie Reference

| Food | Portion | Calories |
|---|---|---:|
| Egg | 1 egg (50g) | 70 kcal |
| Chicken breast | 100g | 165 kcal |
| Cooked rice | 100g | 130 kcal |
| Whole wheat bread | 1 slice | 80 kcal |
| Banana | 1 medium | 90 kcal |
