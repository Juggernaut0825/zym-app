# Training Guide

## Suggested Weekly Frequency

| Experience level | Training days/week | Split guidance |
|---|---:|---|
| beginner | 3 | Full body 3x/week |
| intermediate | 4-5 | Upper/lower split |
| advanced | 5-6 | Push/pull/legs split |

## Rep Ranges and Rest

| Goal | Rep range | Rest interval |
|---|---|---|
| strength | 1-5 | 3-5 minutes |
| hypertrophy | 6-12 | 60-90 seconds |
| endurance | 15+ | 30-60 seconds |
| fat-loss conditioning | 12-15 | 45-60 seconds |

## Prioritize Compound Movements

Recommended foundational lifts:
1. Squat
2. Deadlift
3. Bench Press
4. Row
5. Overhead Press
6. Pull-up

## Weekly Volume Targets per Muscle Group

- Beginner: 10-12 sets
- Intermediate: 12-16 sets
- Advanced: 16-20 sets
